Instructions

You need to guess the hidden animal in the covered image below!! At the start the image is fully covered and no letters are revealed. The game flows as follow:

  1. Guess a letter (case insensitive).
  2. If the word contains the above letter, it'll be permanently displayed at its position, and part of the image will be displayed.
  3. If the word does not contains it, then you lose an attempt and part of the image will be displayed to help you.
  4. Remember that you have limited attempts!! The goal is to find all the correct letters which form the word of the animal showed in the picture!!
